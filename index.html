About us

Since 1996 mixing performance and quality


In the beginning, the main project was based on maximum speed
and comfort motorbikes. After this point, the projects were upgraded with special structures,
materials and durability enhancement, bringing the new prototypes to the market with total force.
Today, the luxury and the powerful engines are core-part of the motorcicles and these aspects
will suffer deep update for the next models.
